Soup Kitchen Basement

Soup Kitchen Basement really is the edgiest of the Northern Quarter clubs. It hosts some crazy events, from heavy metal to pounding techno. It's the place for Manchester's hardcore clubbers.

This is a place that has absolutely no dress code; you could walk in wearing your joggers or your finest suit, nobody would bat an eyelid. In this club, anything goes.

If you're a smoker and you're looking for a nice place to smoke your ciggarettes, this isn't the place. The smoking area is just outside in the alley where the entrance is, and for those hygiene enthusiasts, the toilets are worse than the average club. With the walls consisting of plywood sheets, it really doesn't offer the best of privacy.

If you've lasted this long, you probably want to know about the prices. This is where SKB shines; with cans of beer for sale at a mere £2 with student discount, and single shots of your spirit of choice being sold at £2.50, you're sure to get your buzz!

For anyone wondering about transport, it's not far from Manchester's central bus station which has night busses running to most parts of the city. If you're more of a Taxi person, there's plenty of those around too (especially around 3 - 4am.
