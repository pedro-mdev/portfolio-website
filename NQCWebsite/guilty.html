Guilty

Guilty is the perfect club for the real indie rock lovers. Every night is indie at Guilty, with live DJ's on every open night: Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday.

The dress code is simple: No joggers. Everything else is totally fine. So all of you with retro sports jackets are in the clear.

The entry price is very reasonable at £5 every night but with a £3 discount for students on Fridays. On entry, students will receive a 10% drinks discount voucher if they present their student card. Drinks are a little pricey so it will definitely come in handy.

You'll be paying around £4 per single shot and £5.50 doubles, which is a little better with the discount voucher. Beers and ciders come in at a similar price, but beware the bottles; it costs £5 for a Corona and it'll be served in a plastic cup.
