DIVE NQ

Dive NQ has the latest opening hours of all the clubs in the Northern Quarter, and is even open later than most other clubs in Manchester (outside Canal Street of course).

It's a little stricter on the dress code than the others, but the music is amazing, with a mix of old school classics, indie rock and modern pop; theres something for everyone.

It boasts a large outdoor smoking area, two dancefloors and two seperate bars! It's certainly the priciest of the lot but it's a real bargain on a Saturday, with Â£2 entry for students and massive discounts on drinks (just don't forget your student card!).

For those of you worried about getting home after a long night, there's plenty of options; Picadilly bus station is a short, five minute walk away and for those of you living a little further away, there are plenty of cheap hostels and hotels dotted around the immediate area.
