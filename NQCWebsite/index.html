What is the Northern Quarter?

Scene

The Northern Quarter in the great northern city of Manchester is a location that many flock to from all around the UK. It's a unique place with a significant city, with many calling it the alternative capital of the North. It's home to many small Indie Rock gigs (among others), numerous ALT bars and clubs, record stores and generally places that would'nt be found anywhere else.

Style

A very distinct style can be found in the Northern Quarter: It's a place that is open to anyone and as a result there's a quirky ambience about the place. People dress and look how they want and can feel completely free to do so without judgement. it really does give the place a very unique feel. The open acceptance of grafiti also adds to this, with murals and drawings covering the place, somewhat reminiscent of the Bohemian Hulme as it once was.

Students

Though there is no university in the Northern Quarter, it's a real hub for stuents. With tonnes of cafes for you to study in and bars to go and relax in afterwards, the NQ really is ideal. Additionally, many of these locations offer student discounts!
