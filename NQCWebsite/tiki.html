Tiki

Tiki is the place for the cocktail enthusiasts. It boasts an impressive drinks menu with all sorts of fancy cocktails, even ones with real fire!

This place has some amazing student deals with 2 for 1 Cocktails (Students Only) on both Tuesday's and Sundays! It really is the place you the cocktail conieseurs.

Entry is free every day other than Mondays and Wednesdays (Closed), so it doubles as the perfect place to pre-drink if you're planning to go to a few different places. It gets even better as this venue is placed dead center in the Northern Quarter's main square!

The dress code is very relaxed, with the only restrictions being no Joggers and no sports caps, so all you Vans fans will have no trouble at all.
